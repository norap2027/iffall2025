WHO WE ARE

Overwatch (2016) is a team-based multiplayer first-person shooter by Blizzard Entertainment. Released for PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Windows, and later Nintendo Switch, it features 6v6 matches where players choose unique “heroes” with distinct abilities to complete map-specific objectives. Blizzard added free post-launch content—new heroes, maps, and modes—while cosmetic items were available through optional loot boxes.
